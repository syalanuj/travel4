Markha Valley Trek

Markha Valley is one of the most famous treks of Ladakh Region. It is an amazing trip to get introduced to magical and remote Buddhist Kingdom of Ladakh. While trekking on through this trek tourists passes through beautiful Buddhist Monasteries, Mountain Villages, High altitude pastures of Nimaling and High altitude peak Kangyatse.

Day 1 :
Reach Leh, after flying over some of the most spectacular scenery over the upper himalayas. It is essential to spend the day acclimatising to the rarer air and higher altitude in Leh.
Day 2:
Spend the day at leisure, exploring the main market, and orienting yourself to the bustle of the small Leh town. Nearby attractions include the main market, Shanti Stupa, sun set point, nearby monasteries.
Day 3:
Drive to the road head at Skiu, which is the starting point of your trek. Skiu is known for the copper work of its artisans, and it is worthwhile visiting the small museum here. Cross over the river via a suspended basket pulley cable system to start your trek. Hike a short way to Skiu(3290m), a picturesque village where you camp for the night.

Trek time: 3-4 hours

Day 4:
Trek to Markha(3750m) on one of the longer days of the trek, crossing a river on the way. Once you reach Markha village, you may want to visit the ruins of the old palace here.

Trek time: 6-7 hours

Day 5:
Trek eastwards and upwards to Hangkar(3900m), and the relatively greener landscapes give way to a more austere landscape. Move on and camp at Thachungtse.

Trek time: 7-8 hours

Day 6:
Trek upwards to Nimaling(4720m), a pastoral land sloping upwards to the base of the Kang Yatse mountain. The pastures here provide sustenance to an abundant number of yaks, sheep, goats, Dzos and and horses.

Trek time: 6-7 hours

Day 7:
Cross over the highest point of the trek, the Kongmaru La (5216m) on this day. From the top, you get fabulous views of snow capped peaks, and on a clear day, the K2 on the north western horizon. Descend sharply to the Martselang Valley, crossing the sulphur springs of Chuskyurmo(4133m). O/N Chogdo.

Trek time: 7 hours

Day 8:
End your trek by trekking down a shorter distance to Sumdo(3880m). Your vehicle will arrive here to drive you back to Leh, which is at a distance of 2 hours from here.

Trek time: 3-4 hours

Day 9:
Assisted transfer to the airport.
